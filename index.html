Flagship service · pre-owned genset audit

Know What You're Buying Before You Wire the Money.

The secondary market for gas gensets is opaque. Hour meters get rolled back. "After overhaul" means anything from a full rebuild to a fresh coat of paint. Lessors repossess units with no idea of their residual value. We provide the missing piece: an independent engineering audit.

  1. Borescope inspection Cylinder condition without disassembly
  2. Operating-hours verification Indirect wear evidence vs. meter readings and documentation
  3. Oil and coolant analysis Wear metals, contamination, coolant chemistry
  4. Residual life assessment Hours to next minor/major overhaul per OEM schedule
  5. Restoration budget Itemized parts and labor to serviceable condition
  6. Fair market value opinion What the asset is actually worth in its verified condition

Deliverable: a written report with photo and video evidence — suitable for credit committees, insurance and dispute resolution. Ordered by buyers before payment, by leasing companies and banks for collateral, by insurers before coverage, by sellers to defend the price.

Energy-to-Compute

Stranded Gas → Lowest-Cost Power → Compute.

Flared and stranded gas is the cheapest energy on the planet — and the hardest to use. Our answer: autonomous containerized gas engine plants (2–10 MW) built next to the source, powering compute loads that tolerate remote sites.

Phase 1 — Mining. Interruption-tolerant, satellite-uplink-friendly, fast cash flow. Infrastructure is built AI-ready from day one: power density margin, liquid-cooling provisions.

Phase 2 — AI inference. Where fiber reaches the site, capacity converts to GPU inference workloads with softer SLA than training clusters.

The honest math. In mining, electricity is 70–80% of operating cost — cheap gas is the whole edge. In AI compute, electricity is 10–15% of GPU-hour cost — cheap power alone doesn't win; site rights, uptime engineering and operations do. That is why the durable asset is energy rights plus an operated site, and why we sequence mining before AI. All economics on request, marked indicative.
